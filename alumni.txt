<h3><CENTER><p style="font-family:'Arial'">
It’s me- Poppy Rose! Y’all remember I was dumped at the shelter in January by my BYB owner with my babies, my friend and her two pups? Well- look at me now!!!<br> My kids got really good homes of their own, but I stayed behind. Rescue lady called me a “project”. <br>I got all my fur back! I even got spayed so I don’t have to have puppies every 6 months like <br>I used to. I used to live outside in the yard *all* the time. Rain, heat,<br> snow- didn’t matter. I was outside. My skin was all infected and my fur was bleached and dead and fell out. I got used to the box thing (crate) and I like it. <br>I’m learning what to do with toys. I like the ones they hide treats in the best. I’m kinda a foodie. I also like to hang out with humans.<br> Kids are my favorite. Kids give super good pets and I like pets. Have I mentioned I like pets? <br>Small furry things like cats need to<b> DIE</b>, so if you have one <br>I’m probably not<br> a good fit unless it’s ok if I eat it. I like other chill dogs. I’m best fit for an experienced owner who understands I’m still a bit of a project.<br> I like structure, leadership (so I don’t have to make decisions when it’s scary) and lots of attention. I like my people to stay with<br> me and hang out, so if you like to stay home and chill we should seriously talk about your couch. <br>I hate thunderstorms so they’ll send me with my special shirt <br>I wear when the weather is bad. I’m 5 years old and all my vetting is done.<br>cue Lady is waiting to talk to you about me, so paw <br><a href="mailto:Legacydobermanrescue@gmail.com">Legacy Doberman Rescue Email</a>
and let's make it happen!<br> It’s been fun here and I’ve learned a lot but I know my forever family is out there somewhere! <br>
